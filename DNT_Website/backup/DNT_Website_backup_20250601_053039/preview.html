About Us

We are Taiwan's premier digital nomad community, bridging local culture with global remote work opportunities. Our mission is to empower individuals to embrace location independence while fostering meaningful connections and professional growth.

Our Mission

To create a supportive ecosystem where digital nomads can thrive professionally while experiencing the rich culture and opportunities Taiwan has to offer. We facilitate connections, provide resources, and build bridges between local and international communities.

Our Vision

To establish Taiwan as the premier destination for digital nomads in Asia-Pacific, recognized for its innovative community, comprehensive support systems, and unique blend of traditional culture with cutting-edge technology.
