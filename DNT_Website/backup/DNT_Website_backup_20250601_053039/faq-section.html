Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about joining our digital nomad community

Our application process is simple: 1) Submit your application through our website, 2) Complete a brief interview, 3) Join our onboarding program. We look for passionate individuals who share our values of meaningful connection, value exchange, sustainable growth, and dual value creation. No specific educational background is required - we value diverse experiences and perspectives.

Time commitment varies by role and growth tier. Helpers typically contribute 5-10 hours per week, Team Members 10-20 hours, Core Team 20-30 hours, and Organizers 30+ hours. We understand that digital nomads value flexibility, so we focus on results rather than rigid schedules. All commitments are discussed and agreed upon during the application process.

We believe in fair value exchange. Helpers start with experience and skill development opportunities. Team Members receive project-based compensation. Core Team members get monthly stipends plus profit sharing. Organizers receive full compensation packages including salary, profit sharing, and equity opportunities. All compensation is transparent and tied to value creation and community impact.

Our 4-tier growth system provides clear progression: Helper → Team Member → Core Team → Organizer. Each tier offers increasing responsibility, compensation, and impact. We provide mentorship, skill development workshops, leadership training, and cross-functional project opportunities. Growth is based on contribution, impact, and alignment with our values rather than just time served.

English proficiency is required for most roles as we operate internationally. Mandarin is highly valued but not always required - it depends on the specific role and responsibilities. We welcome team members who are learning either language and provide language exchange opportunities within our community. Cultural bridge-building is more important than perfect fluency.

We embrace the digital nomad lifestyle with flexible work arrangements. Most work can be done remotely, but we also organize regular in-person events, workshops, and co-working sessions in Taiwan. Team members are encouraged to attend when possible, but remote participation is always supported. We use digital tools to maintain strong connections regardless of location.

Every new team member is paired with an experienced mentor. We offer comprehensive onboarding, regular skill-building workshops, leadership development programs, and access to online learning resources. Our mentorship program focuses on both professional growth and personal development, helping you navigate the digital nomad lifestyle while building valuable skills.
