About Us

We are Taiwan's premier digital nomad community, bridging local culture with global remote work opportunities. Our mission is to empower individuals to embrace location independence while fostering meaningful connections and professional growth.

Our Mission

To create a thriving ecosystem where digital nomads can connect, collaborate, and contribute to Taiwan's vibrant tech landscape while exploring the beauty of our island.

Our Vision

To ensure everyone who joins our community activities—whether international digital nomads new to Taiwan or professionals seeking to expand their global perspective—can build meaningful cross-cultural connections and leave with enriched insights, broader networks, and life-changing inspiration.

Development Roadmap 發展路線圖

Our strategic milestones towards building the premier digital nomad community 我們建立頂級數位遊牧社群的戰略里程碑

2024 - Present 2024 至今

Voice Amplification Phase 擴大聲量階段
YOU ARE HERE 目前階段

Building international networks and expanding media exposure to establish our presence in the digital nomad community. 建立國際網絡並擴大媒體曝光，在數位遊牧社群中建立我們的存在感。

  • Established partnerships with 15+ international communities 與15+國際社群建立合作關係
  • Featured in major tech and lifestyle publications 在主要科技和生活方式出版物中獲得報導
  • Built core team of 12 passionate professionals 建立了12名充滿熱情的專業人士核心團隊

2025 Q3 2025 第三季

Deep Growth Phase 深度成長階段

Launch comprehensive growth programs and establish paid course systems with expert instructors. 推出綜合成長計劃並建立由專家講師授課的付費課程體系。

  • Launch premium mentorship programs 推出高級導師計劃
  • Establish paid course systems with expert instructors 建立由專家講師授課的付費課程體系
  • Create specialized workshops for skill development 創建技能發展專業工作坊

2026 Q1 2026 第一季

Expansion Phase 擴展階段

Scale operations and establish Taiwan as the premier digital nomad hub in Asia. 擴大營運規模，將台灣建立為亞洲首屈一指的數位遊牧中心。

  • Launch co-working spaces in major cities 在主要城市推出共同工作空間
  • Form international partnerships 建立國際合作夥伴關係
  • Collaborate with government initiatives 與政府倡議合作
