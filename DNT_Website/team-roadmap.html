Development Phases

Our strategic roadmap outlines key milestones in building Taiwan's premier digital nomad community.

🌱

Phase 1: Foundation

Q1-Q2 2024

Establish core community infrastructure and initial member base.

  • Launch community platform
  • Recruit founding members
  • Establish governance structure
🚀

Phase 2: Growth

Q3-Q4 2024

Expand community reach and develop key partnerships.

  • Launch regular events
  • Partner with local businesses
  • Develop mentorship programs
⭐

Phase 3: Expansion

Q1-Q2 2025

Scale operations and establish Taiwan as a digital nomad hub.

  • Launch co-working spaces
  • International partnerships
  • Government collaboration
👑

Phase 4: Leadership

Q3-Q4 2025

Become the leading digital nomad community in Asia-Pacific.

  • Regional expansion
  • Innovation lab launch
  • Thought leadership
